A sacred space to come home to yourself

Hey there, I’m Janani - but most people call me Jana ♡

I created Just Jana to help other girls like me - girls who grew up shrinking themselves to fit in, who were told to be someone else in order to be accepted.

This space is my way of saying: you don't have to anymore.

Through fitness, music, conscious living, and awareness around mental health, I began to heal and reclaim myself - layer by layer.
And now, I share my lived experiences to help you do the same

If you’ve ever felt disconnected from your voice, body, or your truth...
This is your sacred space to come home to your truest self.

Welcome home. You belong here - just as you are ♡
