A sacred space to come home to yourself

Hey there, I’m Janani - but most people call me Jana 🤗

I created Just Jana for anyone who's ever felt like they had to shrink themselves or be someone else to fit in.

Here, I share my journey of healing through fitness, music, conscious living, and awareness around mental health - to help you reconnect with your voice, your body, and your truth.

This is your space to heal, rise, and be unapologetically you

What Just Jana Stands For


🌸 You Deserve to Be Seen

Your softness, your fire, your story - it all belongs here.

You don’t need to shrink to be loved. You get to take up space.

🧘 Healing Is Personal - there's no one right way

Movement, music and conscious living helped me, but this space invites you to explore what healing looks like for you.

💛 You Are Not Broken

Letting go of shame is the first step toward becoming.

You are not behind. You are becoming - at your own pace.

🌍 A Conscious Sisterhood

We grow together. We unlearn together. We rise together.

Just Jana is your space to begin - and to belong.
